About Our School

Ebenezer Nursery & Primary School

Located in Kasokoso - Kireka, Ebenezer Schools provide quality education through both day and boarding programs. We are committed to creating a nurturing environment where children can grow academically, spiritually, and socially.

Our dedicated staff and modern facilities ensure that every child receives the attention and resources they need to reach their full potential.

With a foundation built on Christian values, we strive to instill moral principles and a heart for service in all our students.

Nursery Program
